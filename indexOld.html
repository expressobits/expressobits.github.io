About

Expresso Bits is a development studio located in Santa Maria, Brazil.

Founded on May 1st, 2015 the company that was designed by the brothers Gabriel and Rafael through its trajectories in the branch.

Our biggest project is the urban mobility app called HBus. We are working on other major projects, but we are still not ready to talk about them. We are always looking for creative risks that can keep our ideal alive.
